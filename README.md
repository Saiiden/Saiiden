## The Journey

During my initial forays of using technology I used to be really interested in understanding how it works. This curiosity ignited a never ending thirst for seeking knowledge from the ever flowing river of knowledge. Initially exploring the ways of the UNIX enabled me to further the understanding of how operating systems work and why I was so bad at using something with bloated and inefficient aesthetic, once it was deserted this paved a new and hard path to a performant, free, efficient, intuitive technology that was hidden all along overshadowed by mere delusion. This along with a group of my peers in freedom furthered into the depths of technology, resulting in the acquiring of multiple programming languages and a few skills that were developed through the participation in Capture The Flag challenges (CTF). In the beginning it was just w3challs that had us addicted to that very feeling of completeness, gradually going across other places such as Hack The Box and TryHackMe. It has been almost 5 years since stepping into technology. I also actively participate in an Indian Linux Group Community to further UNIX and its intuitive ways.
